About Elite Breeders USA

With over 15 years of experience, we are dedicated to breeding healthy, well-socialized animals with exceptional temperaments and conformation.

Our breeding program focuses on genetic diversity, health testing, and proper socialization to ensure our animals become beloved family members.

  • Health tested breeding stock
  • Early neurological stimulation
  • Nationwide shipping available
  • Lifetime breeder support
  • AKC/TICA registered animals
